# Look After Yourself 3000

# Description
Look after yourself 3000 is note taker app with different features. The user will either sign up to a new account or log in which will then take them to the calendar to pick a date where they want to write their notes. After they pick a date, they can write their intake of water for that day, their excesie for the day, if they had a good night sleep, and their overall mood. Then, they will write any to dos for that day or tasks that needs to be done. After doing this, they will then click on a graph button that will display their activies and keep track of their mental health.
